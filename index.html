A bit of who I am

My name is Rohan Jose. I am a 2nd Year student majoring in ITM at Illinois Tech (obviously). Right now, I am living on campus while attending online for all 5 of the classes I'm taking this semester.

Ghana

I grew up in the capital city of Accra, in the heart of Ghana, a very lovely little country situated in West Africa. Growing up has always been a mix of so many cultures for me. Especially since I'm Indian by nationality but spent my entire life experiencing the life of a Ghanaian. In fact, this has created a newfound love for a fusion of these 2 extremely far away yet surprisingly similar cultures. This fusion has pushed me to learn 2 languages since I was a kid - English (the most commonly spoken language in Ghana) and Hindi.
